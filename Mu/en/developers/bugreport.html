Bug Reports

Before reporting a bug, please consider the following:

Check your version

μ is in a state of perpetual development.

As such, if you wish to query or report a bug, you must try with the latest development branch revision of μ to confirm the issue still exists.

Check the bulletin board on Support Center, some users may provides you the solution to your problem.

Writing a report

When writing your bug report, please include (uncompressed):

  • What you were trying to accomplish. (e.g., "I'm trying to play a FLAC file...")
  • The problem you encountered. (e.g., "No sound come out." or "Mu crashed.")
  • Sufficient information, including any required input files, to reproduce the bug and confirm a potential fix.

Report the bug

For common user:

You can report your bug by:

  • Send your bug report via E-mail, our address can get from our Support Center.
  • Leave your bug report on our Support Center. (Not Recommend)

If you are a Github user:

You can just report the bug in our Issues page, it's easy to follow your bug state and get in touch with us.
